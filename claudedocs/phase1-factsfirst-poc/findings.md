Date: 2026-06-13. Scope: `scope.md`. The agent authored a fact-base first,
gate-checked it, then hand-rendered prose as a projection. Live runs against
`factsfirst.atomic.json` (+ a throwaway contrast store).

## Result: the owner's facts-first insight is VALIDATED at the mechanism level —
## the R494 extraction gap dissolves, and FRAMES give clean truth/revelation
## separation (mystery-native). Prose craft stays the deferred blind question.

## The three live results

The Belvoir letter, authored TRUTH-FIRST. Frames `truth` (ground truth) and
`household` (the misdirection). The load-bearing `helene @ study` is authored UP
FRONT in `truth` (because we are building the truth, not extracting it from prose).

1. **The fact-base gate-checks clean, cross-frame divergence = DATA.**
   `validate-continuity` = violations 0, `cross_scope(data)=1`. Within each frame
   consistent (truth: helene @ study; household: helene @ sickroom); the
   truth-vs-belief divergence is frame-scoped → not a contradiction. The
   perspectival model (north-star: agent-belief ≠ ground-truth, both true) carries
   the misdirection without a false alarm.
2. **The dramatic irony is surfaced deterministically.**
   `report-irony-intervals` = `[window, OPEN at world-line end] f-household-sickroom
   (household) vs f-truth-study (truth), opens at sc-murder`. The mystery's engine
   — the gap between what the household believes and what is true — is a queryable
   window, not lost.
3. **Gate power intact: a same-frame error IS caught (contrast).** Placing Helene
   in two locations IN the `truth` frame fires `rule_exclusive_overlap` (exit 1).
   Facts-first does not weaken the gate: cross-frame = mystery (data), same-frame =
   real error (gated).

## Truth vs revelation — hand-rendered prose projections (same base, two frames)

The SAME checked fact-base yields the mystery and the reveal, by choosing which
frame to project. The truth is always present and checked; the prose reveals
selectively.

- **Projection of `household` (the mystery — withholds the truth):**
  > All Belvoir knew Helene had not left the sickroom since the fever; the bolt
  > was drawn each night, and Dr. Marsh kept the only key. Whatever had become of
  > the letter, it could not have been her hand.

- **Projection of `truth` (the reveal):**
  > She had been in the study after all. The bolt, the key, the doctor's
  > certainty — each had been a thing she let them believe. The letter burned
  > because Helene's own hand had burned it.

The first prose NEVER states `helene @ study` — yet the fact is in the base,
checked, and surfaced as irony. Mystery and consistency coexist.

## Why this dissolves the R494 gap

R494: in prose→facts, the load-bearing `helene @ study` was never stated, so a
faithful extraction missed it and the gate was silent. Here, authoring FACTS
FIRST means `helene @ study` is in the base BY CONSTRUCTION (you cannot author
the murder's truth without placing Helene at the scene). There is no lossy
extraction step; the gate validates the base BEFORE any prose. The gap is not
"better extracted" — it is structurally impossible, because the prose is a
projection of the facts, not their source. This re-derives the north-star
architecture thesis (every artifact is a projection of the one substrate) and is
the CYOA-renderer direction (facts → narrative).

## Honest boundaries

- **Hand-rendered**: no automated facts→prose renderer exists (CYOA backlog
  unbuilt); the projections above are by hand, demonstrating the WORKFLOW + the
  mechanism, not an automated pipeline.
- **Prose CRAFT untested**: whether facts-first yields GOOD narrative (or wooden
  list-prose — the scale-floor's craft tension where plain won on preference) is
  NOT testable by a self-authoring agent; it needs the blind, session-separated
  judge (R469). This PoC tests the mechanism + the truth/revelation separation
  only.
- **Fact completeness still authoring-bound**: facts-first FORCES thinking in
  facts (so the load-bearing fact is declared — that is WHY the gap closes), and
  lets the gate check the base before prose; but it does not invent which facts
  are relevant — the relevance ceiling (R476) stands.

## What this advances (past R494)

R494 located the bottleneck in prose→facts extraction (AI under-infers implicit
facts). Facts-first DISSOLVES that bottleneck: author facts first (forced to
declare the load-bearing ones), gate-check the base, render prose as a
projection. So the AI-first authoring loop is not "extract better from prose" —
it is "author the fact-base first (gates checking it live), then project prose."
The remaining hard parts are prose craft from facts (blind judge) and fact
relevance (the R476 ceiling) — not the extraction gap, which is gone by
construction.
