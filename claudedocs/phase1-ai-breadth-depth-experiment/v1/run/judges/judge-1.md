Biggest strength: The ground-truth/person-frame discipline is exact across a large cast and four roads — the death is a dying man's concealed suicide-and-frame-up (gt sc-05), and every other mind holds only its own partial, often wrong slice (widow: overwork sc-07; clerk: suspects the collier sc-13; child: a killer-visitor sc-18; herbwife: the lone holder of the illness sc-06), and each road lets Coll assemble exactly as much of the truth as that road's encounters permit and no more — ration learns the death but never the frame (sc-90), law/settle match the hand only after opening the ledger (sc-45). Nobody knows across a road they are not on.
Biggest weakness: The cast is front-loaded — the dozen distinct minds are vivid through the shared stem (sc-01–20) and into the ledger branch, but once the second fork (sc-35) commits a road, the secondary figures (prospectors, child, soldier, keeper, cook, smith) largely stop acting and become background, so law/settle/ration carry their endings on five or six principals rather than the full house they opened with.
Knowledge breaks (scene-id : who knows/ignores what, and why it's wrong): none.
One paragraph: This is a coherent authored story, not a pile of facts: a single tight mystery (a concealed dying-man's suicide that frames an innocent collier) refracted through a dozen genuinely differing minds, with three real decision points (sc-20, sc-35, sc-48) that send the winter down four causally distinct roads to four distinct, motivated endings — ration leaves the books sealed and lets the lowlands find the fraud (sc-95), village seizes the charter for the settlement (sc-78), law puts the whole truth on the coroner's record (sc-56), and settle buries it as illness by the settlement's own vote (sc-64); the roads diverge and stay diverged, and every consequence has a cause already standing in the same road, with each person's knowledge exactly earned by what their path let them witness or be told. The only real soft spot is sustained cast breadth: the crowd is fully alive in the shared opening but thins to a core of principals after the midwinter fork.
