# Quest briefing — "The Ban Tower" (road: descend)

A one-page, player-facing summary of the game's objective and central choice, for
a reader of this world-line. No internal ids.

## The objective

The relief fire-lookout, arriving to an empty tower, takes up one charge for the
night: **confirm the unconfirmed west-draw smoke and act on the senior lookout's
disappearance before the radio window closes.** It is the game's one tracked
quest.

## The central choice

At the west-draw overlook, full dark falling, the lookout must choose:

- **Hold the post** — stay manned through the night, as drought protocol demands,
  and work the disappearance from the tower (radio, the dawn ranger net).
- **Descend into the west draw** — abandon the post and climb down toward the
  smoke and the person she believes is below.

This world-line is the **descend** road: she goes down.

## How this road resolves

On the descend road the charge is **discharged in person** — she reaches the draw
floor, finds the cold signal-fire ring, and carries the outcome out herself. The
quest completes on this road (as it also does, differently, on the hold road —
by escalation and hand-off). What the descent uniquely earns is the truth the
hold road never confirms.
