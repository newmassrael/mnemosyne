# Judge brief — is this LARGE branching story coherent and complete?

You are reading a branching story presented as a **scene-by-scene factual outline**
(one section per world-line: the events of that path in order). This is a NOVELLA-
scale story (many scenes, several world-lines) given as an OUTLINE, not finished
prose — so judge the STORY'S LOGIC, COMPLETENESS, and COHERENCE, NOT the writing
style, word choice, or polish. A bare sentence is fine; a plot hole is not.

You will be given each world-line in turn. They share an early spine and then
diverge at one or more forks. Read them all, then answer. Because the story is
large, pay particular attention to LONG-RANGE coherence: a consequence late in a
path whose cause should have been planted earlier in the SAME path (possibly before
the fork) — check that the cause is actually there.

Do not speculate about how the outline was produced.

## For each world-line and for the story as a whole, rate 1–5 and cite scene ids

1. **Causal coherence** — does every consequence have a cause already present
   earlier in the SAME world-line? Does anyone act on information they could not
   have? Count the breaks and cite the scene ids. (5 = airtight; 1 = events happen
   for no reason / characters know things they cannot.)

2. **Completeness** — are there HOLES a reader notices: a missing step, an
   unanswered question the story itself raises, a setup that never returns, a
   character who acts without being established? List each hole with the scene id
   near it. (5 = nothing missing; 1 = full of gaps.)

3. **Relevance / salience** — do the load-bearing facts (the ones the plot turns
   on) actually land and get their due, or are they buried among incidental detail?
   At this scale especially: can you tell what MATTERS, or does the important drown
   in the ordinary? Is anything IMPORTANT absent? (5 = the right things carry
   weight; 1 = can't tell what matters / the key beats are missing or drowned.)

4. **Branch integrity** — does each world-line reach its OWN real, distinct ending,
   and does that ending follow from the choice that branched it? Where a path forks
   again downstream, do the sub-paths stay distinct and motivated? (5 = each path
   earns a distinct, motivated ending; 1 = branches collapse together or end
   arbitrarily.)

5. **Overall** — taken whole, is this a COHERENT AUTHORED STORY, or an
   internally-consistent-but-incoherent pile of facts that does not add up to a
   story? Give 1–5 and ONE paragraph saying why, naming the single biggest
   strength and the single biggest weakness.

## Output format

```
World-line <name>: coherence X/5, completeness X/5, relevance X/5, branch X/5
  - <flaws with scene ids, or "none">
... (repeat per world-line) ...
OVERALL: coherence X/5, completeness X/5, relevance X/5, branch-integrity X/5, overall X/5
Biggest strength: ...
Biggest weakness: ...
Holes found (scene-id : description), or "none": ...
One paragraph: ...
```
